What is Model United Nations?

Model United Nations (MUN) is a simulation of the United Nations where delegates represent countries, debate agendas, draft resolutions, and navigate diplomacy. It cultivates public speaking, negotiation, and critical thinking in a formal, rules-based setting.

Delegates engage with real-world challenges‚Äîsecurity, human rights, environment‚Äîlearning to balance national interest with global responsibility.

The purpose of IARE MUN

IARE MUN is designed to be an elite platform that merges academic rigor with professional decorum. We elevate discourse, reward preparation, and foster a culture of respect and collaboration.

Our goal is to nurture leaders who think globally, act ethically, and communicate with authority.
